About

I orginally pursued music as my career, getting a degree in Music Business. After I got my degree, I decided that music was more my passion than something I actually wanted to make a career at. After graduation, I worked full time at a library, which had alot of downtime. I started learning to code when it was slow and became increasingly interested in web development. Two years after starting that job, I decided to quit and throw all of my chips into learning web development and haven't looked back.
I'm proficient in HTML, CSS, and am competent in JavaScript. I'm well versed in using frontend libraries such as jQuery and Bootstrap and am currently learning Vue.js. I also have familiarity with Wordpress and PHP. Take a look at my portfolio down below and see some samples of my work!
