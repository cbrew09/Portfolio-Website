About

While working at a library after graduation, I had alot of downtime and increasingly became interested in coding, specifically web development. I decided to throw all my chips into web development and quit my job to have more time to learn the necessary languages and technologies. I'm proficient in HTML, CSS, and am competent in javaScript. I am able to use technologies such as jQuery and Bootstrap and am currently learning Vue.js. Take a look at my portfolio down below and see some samples of my work!
